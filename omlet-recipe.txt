Ingredients:
 - 1 egg
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon pepper
 - 1 spoon butter

Directions:
  1. In a medium pan, melt butter and stir.
  2. Break the egg and pour its ingredients in a bowl. 
  3. Add salt and pepper.
  4. Stir the contents of the bowl thoroughly..
  5. Pour the contents of the bowl in the pan.
  6. Spread the contents with a spoon.
  7. Filp the omlet, so that the other side also gets cooked.
  8. Cook it till it becomes golen-brown.
